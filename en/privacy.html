5. Amap (Gaode) compliance

For lawful map display and reverse geocoding in China, TeLog uses Amap Open Platform (lbs.amap.com) under PRC surveying and map regulations.

  • Keys are applied through a registered developer account on the official platform, scoped to permitted service types (Web JS API for maps; Web Service API for reverse geocoding).
  • We use WebView + REST (not the native iOS SDK), matching Key types.
  • Data sent to Amap may include GCJ-02 coordinates and map viewport parameters — not your Tesla password or full trip database.
  • Fallback: if Amap is unavailable, coordinates may be sent to OpenStreetMap Nominatim (outside China) when allowed in settings.

Amap privacy: Amap Privacy Policy
